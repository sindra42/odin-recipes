Pink Fluffy Stuff

Savor the Memory of Home

This light and fluffy dessert is a treat that my family would make during the summer. It's an easy no-bake that is sure to be a crowd pleaser at any BBQ or big get together.

Ingredients

  • Angel food cake (store bought)
  • Strawberry Jell-O
  • Boiling water
  • frozen whipped topping (such as cool whip thawed in fridge)
  • frozen strawberries in sugar (the kind that comes with juice, not whole berries), straight from the freezer

Steps

  1. Remove the strawberries from the freezer (if frozen) and let them sit on the counter while working through the next steps.
  2. Crumble up the cake and add half of the pieces to the bottom of a dish.
  3. Mix Jell-O in a bowl with 2 cups boiling water. Stir well to dissolve the jello powder.
  4. Add strawberries immediately and stir. Frozen strawberries work best as the cold helps the jello to set.
  5. Stirring helps to melt the strawberry juice, but if the juice sticks in clumps you may need to use your hands to break them.
  6. Fold in spoonfuls of cool whip until well mixed.
  7. You should take the cool whip out of the freezer to thaw first, but if not it will still work.
  8. Spoon 1/2 of mixture over the first half of angel food cake in dish.
  9. Repeat with a second layer of angel food cake and the rest of the mixture.
  10. Make sure all pieces are submerged in the mixture to soak up the sweet creamy goodness.
  11. Cover then set in refrigerator for several hours.
  12. The longer it sets the more it will keep shape when being served, but you can just spoon it into bowls. Congrats on your pink monstrosity!
